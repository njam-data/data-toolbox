Embeds


This page contains a list of ready-made data visualizations produced by the data team complete with embed codes that allows them to be dropped directly into movable type. This page is under construction and will grow with time. Some visualizations may be templates that need to be updated to include the most recent data, or could be easily utilized for another topic.

If you need help doing this or have questions, please e-mail Carla Astudillo or Stephen Stirling on the data desk or click the "Ask a Question" button at the top.

***Important note: Any embed used in Movable Type must have a width set to "100%" in order to work in mobile.***
